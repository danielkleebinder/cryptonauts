Welcome fellow Cryptonaut

I hope your cross-multiverse travel wasn't too boring. Since you arrived all healthy and not deformed at all I assume everything went well on your trip to the Cryptoverse?

You are a new Cryptonaut. As you have no experience this far, let me quickly introduce you to the game. Our currency are so called space diamonds (SD). There are three options how you can pile up your stock:

  • Mine them during your explorations on hostile and untouched planets
  • Become a space pirate and fight against other players to gather some of their space diamonds
  • ... or you can buy them for Ether in your inventory

To make your explorations and battles more lucrative, take a look into the store in your menu to the right. You can buy items there and upgrade them in your inventory.

Notice: You have to level your cryptonaut to level one to be able to fight and explore planets! This is quite easy, give it a try in your cryptonauts profile.
