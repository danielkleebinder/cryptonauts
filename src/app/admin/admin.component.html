Owners

Owners are accounts with special administrative rights in the game. They can create items, redeem money on the contract or set balancing parameters.

Items

Use this form to create a completely new item type. Players can buy those items and upgrade them to their will. But be careful to not make them too powerful or too weak. Players will abuse this.
